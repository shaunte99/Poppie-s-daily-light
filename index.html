💌 Message to Ms Sunshine

Hey Sunshine 💖 Never lose sight of how sacred your well-being is.. to me to your family and to the One who made you. You were formed with care.. breathed into with purpose.. and are watched over with a love deeper than anything this world can offer. The Lord says He will never leave you nor forsake you.. and that promise was written with you in mind.

Even when the weight feels too heavy or your smile doesn’t come as easily.. you are not alone. The same God who calms the sea walks beside you. And me.. I’m here too.. every step of the way.. loving you with a heart that wants nothing but peace for your soul and joy for your spirit.

I pray protection over your heart.. clarity over your mind.. and rest for your body. I pray that the days ahead are soft with blessings and loud with laughter.. and that no weapon formed against you will prosper. You are too important.. too radiant.. too divinely crafted to be anything less than covered in grace.

You're someone I care about Always.

I always got your back Ms sunshine💖
