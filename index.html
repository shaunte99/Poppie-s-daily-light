💌 Message to Ms Sunshine

Hey Ms. Sunshine 🌸

I hope today treated you with the warmth and grace you deserve... that everything you set out to do went smoothly, and even if it didn’t, I pray it brought you closer to something beautiful.

Today marks exactly two months since I started this page for you. I’ve missed about five days of updates since then and yeah, I know I’ve got to do better 😅. But here’s what’s wild: I thought it would be hard to come up with new words every day… to find the right scriptures, the perfect quotes, or messages to brighten your day. I thought I’d run out. But the truth is, I never do. Every single day, there’s something new I want to tell you... something wonderful I want to give you. Whether it’s a verse from the Word, a quote from one of the books I read, or just a reminder of how incredible you are.. it always flows, because you're always inspiring.

In simple terms, Ms. Sunshine… I’ll never run out of words to express how amazing you are. Not today. Not ever.

I’m endlessly proud of the work you put in every single day. I pray to God that one day you’ll achieve even your wildest dreams. You mean the world to me, and I hope this little page continues to bring you even a little joy. I’ll keep working to make it better...just like I’ll always be in your corner. Forever and always. 💛
