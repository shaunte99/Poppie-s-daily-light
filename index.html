📜 A Letter to Future You

Dear future you,

I already see her the girl who Did everything, who smiled through battles no one saw. The one who never stopped shining.

I hope you’ve fallen in love with your mind, with your gentleness, and your resilience. I hope you know you are loved no matter what you do no matter what you achieve.

Don’t forget how rare your heart is. Don’t let the world make you hard. Keep your softness it’s your superpower. And no matter how far you go, you’ll always have someone who believed in you before you even got there.

I already see the future I hope you do too
