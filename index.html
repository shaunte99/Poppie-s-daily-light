💌 Message to Ms Sunshine

Good evening Ms Sunshine,

Did you know that sometimes clouds can glow silver under the moonlight because of a phenomenon called “moonlit cloud scattering”? Basically, the moon’s light gets bent and diffused by tiny ice crystals or water droplets in the clouds, making them look like floating pieces of silver. I always find it fascinating how something so ordinary in the sky can suddenly feel magical when the conditions are just right.

It kind of reminds me of the world sometimes..how we don’t always have the best days or the best emotions, but even small moments of light can completely change the feeling of everything.For instance Seeing you happy, doing something you love, literally fills my heart. I want you to know that your happiness truly means the world to me.

You are my favourite person in this world, and I will always fight to make sure you’re okay. I’ll always be there for you, no matter what, because I have your back.
