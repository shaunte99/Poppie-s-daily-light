💌 Message to Ms Sunshine

Hey Ms Sunshine 🌸

Ether 12:27 says, "My grace is sufficient... for my strength is made perfect in weakness." It’s not about being flawless, it’s about letting the Lord’s power turn every uncertainty into strength.

I honestly think the reason you feel doubt sometimes is because you’re meant for things that scare the enemy. Once you push past that doubt with faith, you will inspire and lift so many people ...in ways that truly matter.

I need you to know that my belief in you is constant... Whether you’re feeling unstoppable or a little unsure, I see the same amazing woman every time. The Savior walks with you, and I’ll be right there too.

So never underestimate the light you carry. You were created for something far greater than you can imagine right now, and I can’t wait to watch it all unfold. You mean the world to me. 💛
