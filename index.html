💌 Message to Ms Sunshine

Ms Sunshine 💛

I came across a line that made me think of you: “In the middle of every difficulty lies opportunity.” And it made me think of how, no matter what comes your way, you keep shining. Even when things feel tough or confusing, you somehow make it look effortless, like you were always meant to glow. You don’t just get through stuff—you leave a little sparkle behind wherever you go.

Then I read Isaiah 40:31: “But they that wait upon the Lord shall renew their strength; they shall mount up with wings as eagles; they shall run, and not be weary; and they shall walk, and not faint.” And I thought about how you’re not carrying your journey alone. The strength you’ve felt running low? God promises to refill it. The moments you feel like you can’t go further? He says you’ll soar. There’s a whole future waiting for you, and it’s not built on exhaustion—it’s built on divine renewal.

I just wanted to leave you with that tonight. Every obstacle in front of you is just proof that the next level of your glow is loading. You’re not only being shaped—you’re being lifted. You are cared for, deeply loved, endlessly protected… and you mean the world to me.
