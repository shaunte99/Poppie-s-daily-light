💌 Message to Ms Sunshine

Hey Ms Sunshine

I just wanted to take a moment this Tuesday to share something beautiful. Jean Paul Richter once said, *“Do not wait for extraordinary circumstances to do good; try to use ordinary situations.”* I love this because it’s a gentle reminder that even the little things—your smile, your kindness, your presence have the power to bring so much light into the world. You don’t need anything grand to make a difference, because you already are extraordinary in the everyday.

And in Joshua 1:9, God encourages us: *“Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the Lord your God will be with you wherever you go.”* That promise is unshakable. No matter what challenges come your way, you are never walking alone—He goes with you, surrounds you, and lifts you up with His strength.

I want you to feel that today, Ms Sunshine. To know deep in your heart that you are loved beyond measure, protected by God’s hand, and cherished more than words can capture. 💛
