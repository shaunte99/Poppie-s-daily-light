💌 Message to Ms Sunshine

Happy Friday! I came across a line from Paulo Coelho that made me think of you: “And, when you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it.” It reminded me of how every dream you hold, in pageantry, in content, in life, seems like it was written in the stars for you. The world has a way of leaning in your favor because your spirit chases things with such fire.

Then I read Exodus 14:14: “The Lord will fight for you; you need only to be still.” And I thought about how beautiful it is that you don’t have to carry everything on your shoulders. Even in the quiet, even in the waiting, God is already at work for you, lifting battles, opening doors, and guiding your steps.

I just wanted to leave you with that tonight. Your dreams are not just dreams....they are promises waiting to unfold. And you’re not walking toward them alone. You are the universe’s favorite, and the Lord’s beloved.I hope you see it Ms Sunshine 💛
